Yes, we launched on 4/20.

Not for the reason you're thinking — or maybe exactly for that reason, because we are people who love the internet, and the internet has always had a sense of humor about itself.

We picked the date the way you pick a domain name: because it felt right. Because **ter.net.in** is a wordplay hiding in plain sight, and 4/20 is a date that winks at you if you're paying attention.

This is a place on the internet. About the internet. For people who still think the internet is interesting.

We're not sure exactly what it becomes yet. Maybe a blog. Maybe a collection of notes and links and small experiments. Maybe just a blinking cursor with a good domain name.

But it's here. On 4/20, 2026. Because we are like that.
